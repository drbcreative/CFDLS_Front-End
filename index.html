CENTER FOR DERMATOLOGY AND LASER SURGERY

Sacramento Dermatology, Laser Treatments and Skin Care

The Center for Dermatology and Laser Surgery was originally established in 1981 by Emil A. Tanghetti, M.D. as a practice solely devoted to diseases and surgeries of the skin. As his practice has expanded over the past three decades, so has the range of services Dr. Tanghetti now offers. Led by Dr. Tanghetti, the medical staff includes Amanda Knox, Kathy Lemoine, Brynn Gilbert and Rahnie Wood to assure your procedures are performed and supervised by qualified medical personnel.
